Tritax AI is a fully modular bot which comes with sets of features/commands that can be enabled/disabled to your liking, making it customizable exactly how you want. You can turn Tritax AI into a trivia bot, an admin bot, a music bot (...) or all of these together

The default set of modules includes and it's not limited to:

  • Moderation features (kick/ban/softban, filters, mod-log...)
  • Trivia (lists included and you can make new ones!)
  • Music features (playlists, youtube, soundcloud, queues...)
  • Slot machines (yes, really!)
  • And much much more....
  • Just do t!help to get a full list of commands!
